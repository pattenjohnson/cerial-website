The Problem

The gap between training and results.

01

Your athletes are underfeeding their work.

Undereating is silent. It doesn't show up on film — it shows up in the weight room, on the field, and in recovery. Athletes who miss caloric and protein targets are putting in the reps but leaving gains behind.

02

Coaches plan every rep. But have zero nutrition visibility.

You design the program, run the film, and track every metric that matters — except the one variable you can't control in the building. What your athletes eat is the missing piece of every performance plan.

03

Existing apps are too complex. Athletes quit after a week.

Existing apps are databases, not coaches. Athletes search for food, weigh portions, grow frustrated, and stop logging. No data means no accountability. No accountability means no change.
